Welcome to TreeSolutions

TreeSolutions is a full service tree care company within the Dublin region with skilled staff and ISA Certified Arborists on staff. We have served the Leinster Area for over 15 years. We handle all aspects of service from plant health care to hazardous tree removal. We offer both residential & commercial services, and we are Professionally Qualified & insured.

Who are we?

Certified Arborists, dedicated to the highest quality of service and the advancement of Arboriculture. We are a long standing Professional company who have been opperating in the Dublin and surrounding region for more than 20 years. We thrive on a passion for nature and its well being as a living entity in our lives and are contantly looking to preserve it as much as we can. We are also heavily involved with the Irish Tree Climbing Championships and the local ISA chapter.

What do we do?

We provide professional services with quality assessments based on individual situations. No two trees are the same! TreeSolutions know the importance of your trees. Taking care of your trees makes your property safer. adds value, and prolongs the life of the tree and natural enviroment. Mature trees pay dividends by increasing property values, beautifying our surroundings, purifying our air, mediating water runoff, and saving energy by providing cooling shade from the summer heat and buffering the harsh winter winds. Poorly maintained trees can be a significant liability, not to mention and eyesoar! For the safety of your property, you and your trees, speak to an Arborist about your concerns!
