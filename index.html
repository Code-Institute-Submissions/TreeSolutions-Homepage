Who are we?

Certified Arborists, dedicated to the highest quality of service and the advancement of Arboriculture. We are a long standing Professional company who have been opperating in the Dublin and surrounding region for more than 20 years. Our contracts include private homeowners, Dublin City Council, Development Companies and other companies located in different parts of the Country. We thrive on a passion for nature and its well being as a living entity in our lives and are contantly looking to preserve it as much as we can. We are also heavily involved with the Irish Tree Climbing Championships and the local ISA chapter.

What we do?

TreeSolutions know the importance of your trees. Taking care of your trees makes your property safer, adds value, and prolongs the life of the tree and natural enviroment. Mature trees pay dividends by increasing property values, beautifying our surroundings, purifying our air, mediating water runoff, and saving energy by providing cooling shade from the summer heat and buffering the harsh winter winds. Poorly maintained trees can be a significant liability, not to mention an eyesore. For the safety of your property, you and your trees, speak to an Arborist about your concerns!
