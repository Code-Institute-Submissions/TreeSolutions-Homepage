Pruning

Proper pruning is essential in establishing and maintaining a tree with a strong structure and a desirable form. Trees that receive the appropriate attention while they are young will require less corrective pruning as they mature. Pruning must be done with an understanding of how the tree responds to each cut. Improper pruning can cause irreparable damage that will last for the life of the tree, or worse, shorten the tree’s life. Our Certified Arborists are knowledgeable in both the art and science of pruning, and will work to meet your needs and maintain your tree’s health.

Felling

The decision to fell a tree should not be taken lightly. Both from an Enviromental perpective and safety perspective. Felling should only be performed by a qualified company that employs skilled workers who operate in a safe and efficient manner. All of TreeSolutions employees wear appropriate safety gear and are trained to remove trees in difficult situations. We use Cranes and grabs to eliminate time, risk and physical exertion.

Removals

When tree removal is necessary, our team of Certified Arborists are highly skilled in controlled removals of all sizes of trees. Every tree presents a different challenge based on size, species and location. At TreeSolutions, this is our specialization. We are experienced in the use of advanced techniques alongside machinery to safely remove large trees from small and isolated areas. Where conditions permit, we can also provide crane service. Our estimates for removal include the removal of all brush and debris from your yard.

Stump Grinding

Stump removal is an additional service that we offer. The main stump and buttress roots are ground to an average depth of 15”. The result is a mound of soil and wood shavings that we backfill into the depression. We can remove grindings and replace them with soil, new trees or shrubs, grass seed, or straw for an additional charge.
